✦ Coupon Hunter

Never overpay at checkout again.

Coupon Hunter scans the web for working coupon codes, tries them for you, and keeps the one that saves the most — automatically.

100% free & open source — forever
1

Shop like normal

Add things to your cart and head to checkout on any store. No setup.

2

We find & test the codes

The moment a discount box appears, Coupon Hunter hunts 20+ coupon databases (plus codes the store itself advertises) for that exact shop.

3

The best code gets applied

It tries each code, watches your total drop, and keeps the biggest discount. You just check out — and see how much you saved.

Your privacy: No accounts, no tracking, and your cart, payment details, and browsing history never leave your browser. To make shared codes work for everyone, anonymous "this code worked" feedback (just the store + code) is on by default — you can switch it off anytime in Settings.
Open source under the MIT license. Found a bug or a store it misses? Contribute on GitHub.
